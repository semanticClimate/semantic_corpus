# Filtering criteria — climate_anxiety_2026

**Query:** `"climate anxiety" AND (HAS_FT:Y)`
**Total papers screened:** 50
**Included:** 35 | **Excluded:** 9 | **Still under review:** 6

## Inclusion rule
A paper is included if climate anxiety, eco-anxiety, eco-emotions, or climate-related
psychological distress is a **direct subject of study** — not just mentioned in passing.

## Common reasons for exclusion
- Climate/anxiety terms appear only briefly in a list or newsletter, not as the actual
  subject (e.g. PMC13017450, PMC12868119)
- "Climate" used in a non-environmental sense, e.g. "ethical climate" in workplace
  culture research (PMC12892733 — a false positive from the search)
- Paper is about a related-but-distinct construct, e.g. general meteorosensitivity
  rather than climate anxiety specifically (PMC13116603)
- Paper is about climate change's physical/operational effects (emissions, net-zero
  healthcare policy) with no psychological/anxiety component (PMC13151464)
